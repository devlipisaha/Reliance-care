Evidence-Based Assessment

What Is a CANS Assessment?

The Child and Adolescent Needs and Strengths (CANS) tool is a standardized, certified assessment that helps us fully understand a child's situation — identifying needs, strengths, and the right level of care.

At Reliance Care Solutions, we are certified to conduct these assessments to ensure every child's unique circumstances are recognized and a timely care plan is built around them.

1
Initial Intake & ConsultationWe meet with the family to understand the child's background, current situation, and immediate needs.
2
Certified CANS AssessmentOur certified assessors conduct the formal CANS evaluation to document strengths and needs comprehensively.
3
Personalized Care PlanResults are used to build a targeted support plan that addresses each identified need and amplifies existing strengths.
4
Ongoing Review & AdjustmentPlans are reviewed regularly and updated as the child grows and their circumstances evolve.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Which counties do you serve?

Reliance Care Solutions is certified in all 88 Ohio counties by the Ohio Department of Developmental Disabilities. While based in Toledo, we serve individuals across the entire state of Ohio.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept multiple Ohio waiver programs (Individual Options, Level One, SELF, My Life My Choice), Medicare, Medicaid, and private pay. Our team can help determine which options you qualify for.

How quickly can care start?

After an initial consultation and assessment, we work as quickly as possible to match you with a caregiver. For emergency situations — including youth placement — we respond immediately.

Are your caregivers certified?

Yes. All caregivers are background-screened, CPR and First Aid certified, and trained in CPI and PFS protocols. We maintain rigorous hiring and training standards to ensure every client is safe and supported.

Do you offer 24/7 care?

Absolutely. We provide caregiving services 7 days a week, 24 hours a day. We also have an on-call RN available for medical questions and emergencies — help is always just a call away.

What youth services do you offer?

We offer group home services, CANS assessments, emergency respite placement, youth respite care, and animal-assisted therapy. Visit our Youth Programs page for full details.
